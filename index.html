Top three exhibits to visit at the museum

The Davis Family butterfly Vivarium

interact with a room full of a variety of butterflies and moths from all over the world.

The Allison and Roberto Mignone Halls of Gems and Minerals

experience the beauty of over 5000 shiny minerals

The Susan and Peter J. Solomon Family Insectarium

learn about a diverse selection of insects, and how their small bodies contribute largely to our ecosystem
